Manage Actions

    Add New Action

    Step 1: Turn on the toggle switch to activate the extension.

    Step 2: Choose an action from the dropdown menu and click "Save Active".

    Step 3: Add or edit actions as needed under "Manage Actions" .

    Step 4: When you open ChatGPT, your selected action will automatically apply.
    If it doesn`t work instantly, click inside the ChatGPT typing area and press Ctrl + I to activate manually.
    Still not working? Just reload the ChatGPT page once and it’ll work perfectly.
